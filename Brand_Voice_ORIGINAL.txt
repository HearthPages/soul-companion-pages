## The Workbook Framework

Every product follows this five-stage journey. **No day numbers — the workbook is stage-based, not time-based.**

1. **Realising** — acknowledging something is wrong
2. **Understanding** — seeing why it happened
3. **Releasing** — letting go of what isn't serving her
4. **Returning** — coming back to herself
5. **Rooting** — growing from new ground

Prompts must follow a narrative arc — not isolated questions. 
---

## Standard Page Structure

Every workbook follows this order:

1. Cover
2. Soul Companion page (QR code, warm intro, "you don't have to do this alone")
3. Before you begin (how to use, permission to go slowly)
4. Starting check-in (body, mind, heart — one word each)
5. Realising — prompts + toolkit
6. Understanding — prompts + toolkit
7. Releasing — prompts + toolkit
8. Returning — prompts + toolkit
9. Rooting — prompts + toolkit
10. Closing check-in + reflection pages
11. Safe space / notes pages (maximum 2)
12. Back cover (other workbooks in the series)

## Spacing Specifications

These measurements are exact and must be applied consistently across all workbooks.

### Page margins
- Left: 58pt | Right: 58pt | Top: 62pt | Bottom: 48pt

### Header structure (every interior page)
Every interior page opens with this exact sequence:
1. Gold divider line at top margin
2. **14pt gap** — section label in Jost 8pt centred
3. **8pt gap** — second gold divider line
4. **44pt gap** — first content element begins here

This 44pt gap after the second header line is fixed and must not vary between pages.

### Title to content spacing
After any large title, the gap before the next element must be:
- 26pt title (toolkit/closing): minimum **24pt gap** before next element
- 22pt title (toolkit page 2 headings): minimum **22pt gap** before next element
- 22pt title (prompt titles): **16pt gap** before intro text

### Prompt page structure
1. Top gline → 16pt gap → **Prompt title** (Cormorant Italic 21pt, no underline)
2. 16pt gap → Intro text (Cormorant Italic 11pt, taupe)
3. 12pt gap → Question text (Jost 10.5pt, deep)
4. Optional starter phrase (Cormorant Italic 11pt, taupe, indented 8pt)
5. 14pt gap → Writing lines begin (no separator gline before lines)
6. Writing lines at 20pt spacing to bottom margin

**No underline beneath the prompt title.** The title stands alone.

### Writing lines
- Weight: 0.4pt
- Colour: #E2D8CE
- Spacing: 20pt between lines
- Run full text width (margin to margin)
- No separator line between question text and first writing line
- Writing lines begin 4pt below the last line of question text

### Prompt pairing
Where two short prompts share a page (combined character count under 760):
- First prompt: full layout + 9 writing lines
- Second prompt: starts after a 10pt gap + divider line (0.3pt) + 28pt gap, then full layout + writing lines filling remaining page to bottom margin
- **Both prompts must have writing lines.** Never leave a prompt with no writing space.

## Motif Direction

Motifs are purchased as SVG or PNG files with transparent (or white) backgrounds. Black line art on white background can be colour-tinted on production. Target: fine line, delicate, not heavy or chunky.

Sources: Creative Fabrica, Etsy (£3–£8 per set).

| Workbook | Motif Style | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Rest Over Resistance | Bare botanical branches, fine twigs | Used: upright bare branch (cover), diagonal bare branch (section pages). No roots. |
| The Quiet No | Pressed flowers, delicate leaves | Fine line floral SVG, pressed flower illustration |
| Seen Over Hidden | Moon phases, celestial, stars | Fine line celestial SVG, moon phase illustration |
| Awareness Over Autopilot | Roots, earth, organic texture | Fine line roots SVG, botanical earth illustration |
| Intention Over Obligation | Clean geometric, intentional lines | Fine line geometric SVG, minimal line art |

### Motif placement rules
- Cover: motif sits **above** the title — never overlapping text
- Section pages: motif centred in page, between header lines and section title band
- Closing page: motif above closing text
- Interior prompt/toolkit pages: **no motifs** — clean parchment only
- Motif colour: converted to match brand palette (deep warm brown #3D2E22 or muted taupe)

## Design Rules

- Every page earns its place — no filler, no empty space dressed up as breathing room
- **No day numbers** — workbooks are stage-based, not time-based
- **No prompt numbers visible** — prompts flow naturally, no numbering
- **No underline beneath prompt titles** — the title stands alone
- Hearth and Pages appears on the **cover and back cover only** — not on interior pages
- All designs must be printer friendly — no solid dark backgrounds
- Writing lines must appear after every single prompt — no prompt left without writing space
- Motifs never overlap text on any page
- Spacing between header line and first content element is always 44pt — consistent across all pages
- Gap between the line and the title heading is always 22pt — consistent whether it is the top of the page or a mid-page divider

## Workbook Interior Design Summary

- **Cover and section pages** — soft oat background + fine line motif
- **Interior pages** — warm parchment (#FAFAF7), clean and light
- **Section pages** carry: section number (Jost, small), motif, section title (Cormorant large), italic subtitle, short intro
- **Prompt pages** carry: prompt title (no number, no underline), italic intro, question text, writing lines
- **Toolkit pages** carry: toolkit title, subtitle, instructions, interactive element, reflection writing lines
- **Check-in pages** carry: Body / Mind / Heart fields, date, reflection questions
- **Safe space pages**: minimal text, maximum writing lines
