## Next Steps: Adding QR Code to PDF

### Step 1: Generate QR Code

1. Go to **qr-code-generator.com**
2. Paste this URL:  
   ```
   https://hearthandpages.netlify.app/?journal=soul-quiet
   ```
3. Generate the QR code
4. Download as PNG (name it: `soul-quiet-qr.png`)

### Step 2: Add to The Quiet No PDF

1. **Open:** The Quiet No workbook (Word or PDF editor)
2. **Find:** "How to Use This Workbook" page
3. **Add:** At the bottom of the page, copy this text:

---

**Your Soul Companion**

This workbook comes with a free AI Soul Companion — for the moments when you want to explore what's underneath your people-pleasing patterns, or when you're learning to hear your own voice again.

Open your workbook to the section you're on. Scan the QR code below and select where you are. Use the companion to think through what you're noticing — then return to the page and write what you found.

Reclaiming your voice is not loud or dramatic. It's quiet, steady, and real. The companion is here to help you find the words.

*[INSERT QR CODE HERE]*

---

### Step 3: Insert QR Code Image

**In Word:**
1. Click where you want the QR code
2. Go to **Insert → Pictures → This Device**
3. Select `soul-quiet-qr.png`
4. Resize to approximately **3cm square**
5. Center on the page

**In PDF editor:**
1. Use your editor's image insertion tool
2. Insert the QR code PNG
3. Resize to **3cm square**
4. Center it

### Step 4: Export & Upload

1. **File → Save As → PDF**
2. Name it: `The_Quiet_No_People_Pleasing_Updated.pdf`
3. Re-upload to Etsy
